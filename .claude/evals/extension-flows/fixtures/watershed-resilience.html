Watershed Resilience Microgrant

Deadline: August 5, 2026

The Watershed Resilience Microgrant funds teams that monitor stormwater, restore riparian habitats, and teach residents how local water systems work.

Applicants should show a simple workplan, a partner list, and a community reporting habit that makes the results easy to reuse.

Projects that connect youth education, open data, and neighborhood adaptation plans are especially competitive.
